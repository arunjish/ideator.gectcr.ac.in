WORKSHOPS


Workshops are conducted every academic year. The main workshops include Embedded systems, App development, Robotics, Hacking etc.. Students are offered these workshops free of cost.

PROJECT SUPPORT


In order to meet the core aim of Ideator, to nurture the element of innovation among our students and to give them any possible assistance, Ideator conducts project presentation programmes where students are free to present their ideas and technically backed up projects to Ideator panel members and based on pre decided criteria the projects will be selected.

×

THE IDEATOR LAB


The ideator lab can be utilized by students for their project development.Both academic projects and hobby projects are supported by Ideator. The Ideator lab has a very large collection of tools which can be used by the students for their project needs.For using the Lab students should apply online and acquire permission from Ideator.
